Getting and Cleaning Data: Completed with 97%, demonstrating my ability to obtain and read data from a range of sources, formats and file types (web, APIs, databases using MySQL), transforming raw and messy data by using R to handle missing values, organize, merge and manage it in a reproducible way. Reproducible Research: Completed with 97.2%. Interpretable results are the final step in a series of often complex processes. Can the results be reproduced by others? How did we get there? Statistical Inference: Completed the course with 98.3%. Showed my ability to draw conclusions about data populations in a two-part project.
